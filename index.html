Tribute Page to Margie Olbinado, PhD

Dr. Margie Olbinado is an Industrial Liaison Scientist at the Paul Schrerrer Institute in Switzerland. She belongs to the X-ray Tomography Group at PSI since 2019. Margie is an expert in X-ray phase-contrast imaging and tomography, time-resolved X-ray tomography. She is the first Filipino to join the European Synchrotron Radiation Facility (ESRF), where she worked initially as a postdoctoral fellow before becoming a Junior Scientist.
  • 2019 Joined Paul Schererer Institut as an Industrial Liaison Scientist
  • 2015 Joined the European Synchrotron Radiation Facility as PostDoc Fellow
  • 2013 Earned her Doctor of Engineering degree from the University of Tokyo
  • 2010 Moved to Japan on a Monbugakusho Scholarship
  • 2004, 2006 Completed her Bachelors and Masters degrees in Physics from the University of the Philippines Diliman
