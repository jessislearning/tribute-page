Tribute Page to Margie Olbinado, PhD

Brief Bio

Dr. Margie Olbinado is an Industrial Liaison Scientist at the Paul Schrerrer Institute in Switzerland. She belongs to the X-ray Tomography Group at PSI since 2019. Margie is an expert in X-ray phase-contrast imaging and tomography, and other X-ray related techniques. She is the first Filipino to join the European Synchrotron Radiation Facility (ESRF), where she worked initially as a postdoctoral fellow before becoming a Junior Scientist. Her body of work is a testament to her exemplary talent and discipline.

Timeline

  • 2019 - present Joined Paul Schererer Institut as an Industrial Liaison Scientist
  • 2015 - 2019 Joined the European Synchrotron Radiation Facility as PostDoc Fellow and later becoming a Junior Scientist
  • 2013 - 2015 Postdoc researcher for Tohoku University
  • 2010 - 2013 Completed her Doctor of Engineering at the University of Tokyo under the Japanese Goverment Scholarship (Monbugakusho)
  • 2004, 2006 Graduated with Bachelors and Masters degrees in Physics from the University of the Philippines Diliman
